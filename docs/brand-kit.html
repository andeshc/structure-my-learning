The Logomark

For favicons, avatars, and tight spaces.

Three steps, one idea.

Three rounded modules ascending left to right. The modularity reads as structure; the upward climb reads as learning progression — a course, a path, a roadmap. The gradient deepens toward the base, so the climb visibly brightens as it rises.

08

Usage

Keep it consistent — the system only works if it's respected.
✓ Do
  • Use the primary lockup wherever space allows.
  • Give the logo its full clear space.
  • Switch to the mark for favicons and tight spots.
  • Use the dark variant on dark surfaces.
  • Keep the supplied colors exact.
× Don't
  • Recolor outside the palette or re-gradient the mark.
  • Stretch, squash, rotate, or skew any part.
  • Add drop shadows, glows, or outlines.
  • Change spacing between the mark and wordmark.
  • Place the logo on a busy or low-contrast background.
